Discover Jammu and Kashmir

Explore the diverse districts of Jammu and Kashmir, each with its unique charm and cultural significance.

SRINAGAR

Paradise on Earth

Srinagar is famous for its beautiful houseboats and gardens. The Dal Lake is a major attraction.

JAMMU

City of Temples

Jammu is known for its temples, particularly the Vaishno Devi shrine, attracting millions of pilgrims.

ANANTNAG

Land of Springs

Anantnag is known for its numerous springs and beautiful gardens, making it a scenic destination.

BARAMULLA

Gateway of Kashmir

Baramulla is known for its picturesque beauty and historical significance as the ancient gateway to Kashmir.

BUDGAM

Valley of Gardens

Budgam is renowned for its lush green landscapes and beautiful gardens, including the famous Mughal Gardens.

BANDIPORA

Land of Lakes

Bandipora is famous for its stunning lakes like Wular Lake, one of the largest freshwater lakes in Asia.

GANDERBAL

Gateway to the Mountains

Ganderbal is known for its beautiful landscapes and serves as a gateway to the famous Sonamarg Valley.

KULGAM

Scenic Beauty

Kulgam is known for its breathtaking landscapes and is a popular destination for nature lovers.

KUPWARA

Majestic Mountains

Kupwara is known for its stunning mountain ranges and lush green meadows, making it a picturesque location.

PULWAMA

Saffron Valley

Pulwama is renowned for its saffron fields and beautiful gardens, earning it the nickname 'Saffron Valley.'

SHOPIAN

Fruit Basket of Kashmir

Shopian is famous for its fruit orchards, including apples, pears, and cherries, contributing to its reputation as the fruit basket of Kashmir.

KATHUA

Land of Temples

Kathua is known for its historical temples and rich cultural heritage, attracting devotees and history enthusiasts alike.

SAMBA

Historic Forts

Samba is known for its historic forts and temples, reflecting its rich cultural and historical significance.

UDHAMPUR

Mountain Beauty

Udhampur is renowned for its stunning mountain scenery and lush green landscapes, making it a popular destination for nature lovers.

REASI

Spiritual Haven

Reasi is known for its spiritual significance and beautiful temples, including the revered Vaishno Devi shrine.

RAMBAN

Adventure and Nature

Ramban is known for its adventure tourism and natural beauty, attracting those seeking both thrills and relaxation.

DODA

Cultural Richness

Doda is known for its rich cultural heritage and diverse traditions, offering a glimpse into the region's history and culture.

KISHTWAR

Land of Sapphire

Kishtwar is renowned for its sapphire mines and beautiful landscapes, including the stunning Kishtwar National Park.

POONCH

Historical Significance

Poonch is known for its historical sites and scenic beauty, offering a blend of culture and nature.

RAJOURI

Land of Forts

Rajouri is known for its historic forts and rich cultural heritage, making it a fascinating destination for history enthusiasts.
